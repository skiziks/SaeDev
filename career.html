Career

Full Stack Developer

This job includes 2 other jobs at the same time, the Front End developer who takes care of all the part visible to the user, everything we see on a site when we visit it, it uses as programming languages HTML, CSS and Javascript.
The Full Stack job also includes the job of Back-End developer, this job is to manage all the user data, it manages the databases and makes the link between server and client.
He uses PHP, Ruby, Python and SQL as languages.
The profession of Full Stack developer corresponds to those who like to realize their projects in autonomy because he has all the necessary skills to make sites, applications in its totality.

Professional Cameraman

Behind the camera or with the camera on the shoulder, the cameraman films.
As an image technician, he or she works under the direction of the director and the director of photography, who define the angle of the shot, the framing, but also the place and movements of the camera in relation to the subject.
The cameraman coordinates the horizontal and vertical movements of the camera and the characters, in order to reproduce the director's idea as accurately as possible.
His work depends on the project (long or short film) and the director.

Level Designer

It is from the gameplay (concept and rules of the game) elaborated by the game designer that the level designer conceives the levels, that is to say the successive stages that the player will go through in the video game.
The level designer's mission is to imagine and build the player's path by varying the difficulty and ensuring the diversity of the game. The objective is to guarantee a strong playful experience by keeping the player in the heart of the universe proposed by the video game.

Game Developer

His role is to ensure that each element of a specification is followed to the letter. He must be attentive to all aspects of the game: dynamism, playability, quality, etc. His objective is to offer the best quality of game to the users.
His or her missions are therefore numerous and consist of developing gameplay tools, coding existing or new systems and keeping a constant watch on innovations and changes in the environment.
The game developer has a thorough knowledge of game engines and may be required to perform maintenance by ensuring that the program in place is working properly and by correcting any incidents, errors or problems.
